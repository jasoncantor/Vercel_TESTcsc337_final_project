Help Page

Welcome to the Golf Course Management System. Here you can manage tasks, special tasks, budget, and equipment. Use the navigation buttons to switch between different sections. If you have any questions, please contact support.

Login

To log in, enter your username and password in the login form and click the "Login" button.

Job Board

In the Job Board section, you can view, create, and manage tasks. Use the form to create a new task and the table to view and manage existing tasks.

Special Task Tracker

In the Special Task Tracker section, you can view, create, and manage special tasks. Use the form to add a new special task and the table to view and manage existing special tasks.

Budget Tracker

In the Budget Tracker section, you can view, create, and manage budget entries. Use the form to add a new expense and the table to view and manage existing budget entries.

Equipment Tracker

In the Equipment Tracker section, you can view, create, and manage equipment entries. Use the form to add new equipment and the table to view and manage existing equipment entries.
